Notehub

Inspired by learning through Zoom, I built a note-taking application that features a stopwatch and records timestamps of when each note is taken during your live lesson. Use the timestamps while you're studying to reference specific portions of your Zoom recordings. Also, make your notes publicly available to ensure that your absent classmates don't miss a beat!
